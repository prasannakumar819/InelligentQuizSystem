Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a subfield of AI that focuses on interaction between computers and human language.

Key components:
- Syntax: Structure of sentences
- Semantics: Meaning of words and sentences
- Pragmatics: Context and intent

Important techniques:
- Tokenization: Breaking text into words or sentences
- Stemming & Lemmatization: Reducing words to root form
- Part-of-Speech Tagging: Identifying nouns, verbs, etc.

Models used:
- Bag of Words (BoW)
- TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency)

Applications:
- Spam detection
- Chatbots
- Search engines
