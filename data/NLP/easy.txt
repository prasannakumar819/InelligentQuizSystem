Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of Artificial Intelligence that helps computers understand and process human language.

It allows machines to read, understand, and respond to text or speech.

Common applications:
- Chatbots
- Voice assistants (like Alexa, Siri)
- Language translation (Google Translate)

Basic tasks:
- Text classification
- Sentiment analysis (positive or negative opinion)
- Tokenization (splitting text into words)

Example:
If a user types "I love this product", NLP helps identify it as a positive sentiment.
