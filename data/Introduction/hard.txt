Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a broad field focused on creating intelligent agents capable of perceiving, reasoning, and acting.

Key domains:
- Machine Learning (ML)
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Computer Vision

Types of learning:
- Supervised Learning
- Unsupervised Learning
- Reinforcement Learning

Challenges:
- Data quality and bias
- Explainability
- Ethical concerns

Advanced topics:
- Knowledge representation
- Search algorithms (A*, BFS, DFS)
- Optimization techniques
