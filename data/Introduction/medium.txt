Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to systems that can perform tasks such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

AI can be divided into:
- Narrow AI: Designed for specific tasks (e.g., chatbots)
- General AI: Hypothetical systems with human-like intelligence

Important concepts:
- Machine Learning: Subset of AI that learns from data
- Neural Networks: Inspired by the human brain
- Algorithms: Step-by-step procedures for solving problems

Applications:
- Healthcare (disease prediction)
- Finance (fraud detection)
- E-commerce (recommendation systems)
