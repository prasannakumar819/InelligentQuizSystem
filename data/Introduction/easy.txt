Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. 
These machines are programmed to think, learn, and make decisions.

AI is used in many applications like voice assistants, recommendation systems, and chatbots.

Key concepts:
- AI: Machines performing tasks that require human intelligence
- Automation: Performing tasks automatically
- Data: Information used to train AI systems

Examples:
- Siri and Alexa
- Google Search recommendations
- Self-driving cars (basic level understanding)
